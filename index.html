About

I am an aspiring Web Developer whose interest in coding was sparked as a pre-teen spending too much time customizing themes on MySpace. My background is in apparel design and I loved working for small clothing brands during my first few years out of college. I have experience in customer service, manufacturing, fulfillment, digital content management, and digital newsletter design and execution. I have decided to add computer programming to my set of skills with the goal of building beautiful websites as a front-end developer.

Other passions include gluten-free cooking, making lots and lots of playlists, reducing waste in landfills, and Sudoku.
